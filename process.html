Your artwork is printed using the following:

Arches Aquarelle, acid-free, archival quality, 0.53mm 310gsm 100% cotton rag paper. Arches Aquarelle is the only paper mill in France that still uses the traditional cylinder mold process to produce its papers.

Using archival quality Epson Ultrachrome HDX ink designed to resist fading and provide long-lasting prints. This pigment-based ink delivers rich, deep blacks and smooth gradations while offering exceptional color accuracy.

Each print comes with a Certificate of Authenticity that is signed and numbered, ensuring the unique identity and limited nature of your artwork. This certificate confirms that your print is an original fine art piece created with the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
