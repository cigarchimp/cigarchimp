Arches Aquarelle, acid-free, archival quality, 0.53mm 310gsm 100% cotton rag paper. Arches Aquarelle is the only paper mill in France that still uses traditional cylinder mold process to produce its papers. Using archival quality Epson Ultrachrome HDX ink designed to resist fading and provide long-lasting prints. This pigment-based ink delivers rich, deep blacks and smooth gradations while offering exceptional color accuracy.

All of our prints come with their own Certificate of Authenticity, which is signed and numbered by the artist. This certificate guarantees the exclusivity and the quality of your art piece, ensuring that each print is a limited edition and has been crafted with the highest standards of artistry and materials.
